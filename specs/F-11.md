## F-11 Flow 2 — Returning (End-to-End) `[TODO]`

The complete path a user takes when returning to check on their parked car.

---

### F-11.1 Open App With Saved Spot

**Tests**
- GIVEN a spot was saved in a previous session, WHEN the app opens, THEN it enters `"parked"` state (no browsing flash).
- GIVEN parked state, THEN the map centers on the saved spot.
- GIVEN parked state, THEN the saved spot marker is visible on the map.

---

### F-11.2 No Nearby Signs — Clear

**Tests**
- GIVEN no active signs within 150 m of the saved spot, WHEN the app loads, THEN a green "You're clear" banner is shown.
- GIVEN the green banner is shown, THEN no warning sign cards appear below the map.

---

### F-11.3 Nearby Active Signs — Warning

**Tests**
- GIVEN one active CONSTRUCTION sign within 100 m of the saved spot, WHEN the app loads, THEN a red warning banner is shown.
- GIVEN the warning banner, THEN it displays the sign reason, address, and a countdown.
- GIVEN the sign ends in exactly 3 hours, THEN the countdown reads "3h 0m".
- GIVEN the sign ends in 45 minutes, THEN the countdown reads "45m".
- GIVEN two nearby signs, THEN sign cards for both appear below the map, ordered by severity.

---

### F-11.4 Clear My Spot

**Tests**
- GIVEN parked state, WHEN CLEAR MY SPOT is tapped, THEN the spot is removed from localStorage.
- GIVEN CLEAR MY SPOT is tapped, THEN the app transitions to `"browsing"` state.
- GIVEN CLEAR MY SPOT is tapped, THEN the spot marker is removed from the map and all active signs are shown as pins.
- GIVEN CLEAR MY SPOT is tapped and the app is closed and reopened, THEN it starts in `"browsing"` mode.
